Time for Editors-in-chief of Nature



     Word-cloud portrayals of Sir John Royden Maddox. a and b portray the positive and negative sentiment words extracted from the SentiWordNet lexicon, respectively. The results show that the negative sentiment of these editorials is determinant. As a case in point, Maddox penned serial hard-hitting editorials to scold molecular biologists regarding the philosophy of molecular biology.
